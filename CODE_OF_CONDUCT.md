# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Kriptic exists to help people stay safe under genuinely high-stakes conditions. We hold ourselves and this community to a standard consistent with that: contributors, maintainers, and anyone participating in project spaces treat each other with respect, regardless of experience level, background, nationality, or how they showed up to the project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in code review, issues, and discussion.
- Assume good faith; ask clarifying questions before assuming bad intent.
- Respect that some contributors may be working under real personal risk related to the subject matter this project addresses — be thoughtful about what you ask people to disclose about themselves, their location, or their involvement in protest movements.
- Give credit where due, especially for content contributions (legal/medical review) that require real expertise.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Deliberately introducing insecure code, backdoors, or content designed to mislead users about safety-relevant information (legal rights, first aid, marker/SOS behavior).
- Publicly identifying or attempting to identify another contributor who participates pseudonymously, without their consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, reviews, or project spaces.

## Scope

This applies within all project spaces (GitHub repo, issues, PRs, project chat) and in any public space when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report violations to [maintainer contact — add address]. Maintainers will review and respond, and may take any action they deem appropriate, up to and including removal from the project, for violations of this code.
